The capital of France is Paris. Paris is located in the northern central part of France, along the Seine River.

France is a country in Western Europe. It is the largest country in the European Union by land area.

Paris is known for its art, culture, and history. Famous landmarks include the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral.

The city has a population of over 2 million people in the city proper, and over 12 million in the metropolitan area.

French is the official language of France, and the currency is the Euro.
